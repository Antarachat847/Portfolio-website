I had the privilege of collaborating with Cadbury on their Diwali packaging for their e-commerce platform,
a creatively fulfilling and inspiring experience. The project celebrated togetherness,
a cornerstone of Diwali, through a unique card game designed to foster family connections
as well as custom box packaging which included a picture frame, goodie bag filled with choclates as well as a
personalised message making this the perfect gift to celebrate this festival of lights and unity.
